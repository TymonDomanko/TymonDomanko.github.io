#####31-12-2006

##2006 NSW Rover Ball

Last Saturday the 2006 NSW Rover Ball was held at Cataract Scout Camp. The theme was 1950s and 1960s Rock 'n' Roll. Epping Rovers dressed so authentically that they won a heater as a prize. Hardly a more appropriate prize as winter hit Cataract.

*The Weekly Times, 2006*
